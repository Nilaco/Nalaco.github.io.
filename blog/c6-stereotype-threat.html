I cannot recall any moments where I have felt a stereotype threat nor can I recall a time where I noticed or felt someone else was experiencing this. It's as simple as not judging people before knowing them, and not judging yourself because of others. Rather than comparing yourself to others, compare yourself to you X years ago. Problem solved. Judge yourself based on progression rather than the thoughts of others. With that, you won't alter your behavior due to perceptions.
